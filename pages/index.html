Arthur Schnitzler’s diaries online

Arthur Schnitzler, one of the key figures of the Wiener Moderne, meticulously kept diaries from the age of 17 until two days before his death. This website makes the full text of the diaries accessible, which amounts to almost 8,000 pages.
